OUR JOURNEY

From a small idea to a global learning community

SkillSpark was born in 2020 when our founder, Sarah Chen, realized that traditional learning was too slow and expensive. She envisioned a platform where anyone could learn practical skills in minutes, not months.

What started as a small YouTube channel with 10 free coding tutorials has grown into a global platform with over 600 skills, 50,000+ learners, and a community that spans 50+ countries. Today, we're proud to help people from all walks of life ignite their potential.

Our Mission

To democratize skill learning by creating bite-sized, practical lessons that fit into anyone's busy schedule. We believe that learning should be accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of their background.

Our Vision

A world where learning is a daily habit, not a chore. Where anyone can pick up a new skill in minutes and apply it immediately. We envision a global community of lifelong learners who continuously grow and adapt.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at SkillSpark

Speed

We deliver skills in minutes, not hours. Every lesson is designed for quick wins and immediate application.

Practicality
